Why do we miss sometimes?

Given just three weeks, we only had time to train our model with 3 types of weapons (knives, handguns, and large guns which include rifles, shotguns, SMGs, and snipers) on only 812 labeled images. To increase the data set, we used augmentation including techniques such as rotation and shearing in order to extract more information to train our model on. Although it may sound like we have a lot of data, many accurate neural networks have a much bigger data set, going up to millions of photos. If we have more time, we can improve our model performance!
